The problem:

Man, why isn’t there a one line command to see the correlation in the data?
— Some social sciences dude

Why is it a problem?

Well, obviously the problem lies in the word correlation. Every 2nd year student can tell you that what is referred to is actually association, and that its measure depends on the type of variables at hand - nominal, ordinal, interval or scale.
